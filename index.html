ABOUT US

The Sports Committee of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology (S.P.I.T) is a dedicated group of individuals passionate about promoting physical activity and sportsmanship on campus.
Comprising enthusiastic students, they organize a wide range of sporting events, tournaments, and fitness initiatives throughout the academic year.
Their mission is to foster a vibrant sports culture, encouraging students to stay active, build camaraderie, and showcase their athletic talents.
Through their efforts, the Sports Committee plays a pivotal role in enhancing the overall student experience at S.P.I.T.
